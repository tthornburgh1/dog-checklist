Checklist for New Dog Owners

Congratulations on your new dog! You've entered a new world of fun, responsibility, and companionship that will hopefully give you a new lease on life.

Before you freak out, let's make sure you have everything you need to ensure you and your dog are set.

Food (make sure to get it age-appropriate)
An identification tag in case he or she gets lost
Collars, leashes, and a harness
A variety of different kinds of toys
A crate (if crate training) and bed
Basic grooming supplies(brush, toothbrush and toothpaste, shampoo, nail clippers)
Training classes
A vet visit to see if he/she has everything squared away medically
Pads to clean up after accidents
A variety of treats for training
Bags to clean up
